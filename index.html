Privacy Policy

Application: Cleanup Crew
Operator: Sheehan CPA
Last updated: February 2026

1. What This Application Does

Cleanup Crew is a desktop application used internally to batch-import and manage categorized accounting transactions from Excel spreadsheets into QuickBooks Online on behalf of clients who have explicitly authorized access. It also supports querying and permanently deleting transactions from QuickBooks Online.

2. Data We Access

  • QuickBooks Online OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens obtained through Intuit's standard authorization flow.
  • QuickBooks Online accounting data (chart of accounts, vendors, customers, transactions) necessary to validate, post, and manage records.
  • Excel files provided manually by the operator — not uploaded anywhere.

3. How Data Is Stored

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine only, optionally encrypted using Windows DPAPI.
  • No financial data, transaction records, or client information is transmitted to any external or third-party server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is collected.
  • Application logs are stored locally in the installation directory and are not transmitted anywhere.

4. Transaction Deletion

Cleanup Crew includes a Bulk Delete feature that allows permanently deleting transactions from QuickBooks Online by date range and type. Deletions are executed directly against the QuickBooks Online API using your authorized credentials. Deleted transactions cannot be recovered. The operator is solely responsible for ensuring deletions are intentional and authorized by the account holder.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose any data to third parties. The only external service this application communicates with is QuickBooks Online (Intuit Inc.), using your authorized credentials.

6. Data Retention

Local token files can be deleted at any time by revoking access within the application or directly from your Intuit account at accounts.intuit.com.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your QuickBooks data at any time through your Intuit account settings. Revoking access immediately invalidates all stored tokens.
